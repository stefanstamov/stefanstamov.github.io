# About me

Hi, I’m Stefan — a **Cloud Infrastructure Engineer** and **Cloud Infrastructure Architect** with a Master’s degree in Cloud Computing and Computer Science, based in European Union.I design, build, and troubleshoot modern cloud and datacenter environments, with a strong focus on VMware Cloud Foundation, Kubernetes, and secure, automated infrastructure.

Over the past 10+ years I’ve worked across technical support, training content development, professional services, and site reliability engineering.I’ve helped customers and teams run business‑critical workloads on VMware, Tanzu, Proxmox, and open‑source stacks, from greenfield designs and complex upgrades to incident response and performance tuning.

## What I do

- Design and implement VMware Cloud Foundation and vSphere environments, including lifecycle management, upgrades, and integrations with NSX, Aria, and Tanzu.
- Build and operate Kubernetes and container platforms (Tanzu, TKGI, TKG, Docker, Proxmox, Incus/LXD) with a focus on reliability, observability, and security.
- Troubleshoot complex issues across networking, storage, and virtualization layers, often in multi‑cloud and hybrid scenarios.
- Develop technical education: labs, training courses, and certification content for cloud, Kubernetes, and security products.  
- Mentor engineers and teams through workshops, enablement programs, and hands‑on sessions.

## Background

I currently work as a Senior Site Reliability Engineer & Consultant (VCF) at evoila, designing and operating automated VCF‑based cloud platforms.
In parallel, I help part-time with the operations of Consult IT Solutions as a freelance engineer and educator, helping organizations with architecture, implementation, and ideas for upskilling their technical teams.

Previously I have:

- Built and maintained training labs and infrastructure for cybersecurity products, vSphere, and Proxmox at Proofpoint.
- Developed global training and certification content for the VMware Tanzu portfolio as a Staff Technical Content Developer.
- Supported enterprise customers running VMware NSX and Tanzu platforms in production, working on complex escalations and automation for support teams.

I hold a Master’s degree in Cloud Computing from Munster Technological University and multiple certifications including Broadcom/VMware Cloud Foundation Architect, Administrator, VCAP Data Center and VCIX NSX. I have helped with creating multiple certification exams in VMWare and Proofpoit. I heped with the organization of Proofpoint Protect education labs and exams in Austin, TX, New York, NY and London, UK. I have also been recognized as a vExpert and vExpert NSX for my contributions to the VMware community.
